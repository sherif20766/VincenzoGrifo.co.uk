PROFILE

Salve! My name is Vincenzo Grifo, a versatile and bilingual international business and marketing professional from Italy.


I’ve lived, worked and studied in three different countries (Italy, United Kingdom and China), and gained work experience in data analysis, reporting and project management in a variety of industries. I’ve recently completed my second masters in International Business, specialising in Chinese Business and Society and I’m now eager to take on the next challenge and kick start an international commercial career.


My passion is split between my love for constantly broadening my knowledge of International Business Strategy and Marketing, and my drive to use business intelligence and lateral thinking to deliver innovative and effective problem-solving solutions. My professional interests include brand management, merchandising, global expansion, sustainability and global sourcing. I follow with a particular interest the FMCG and Fashion industries, as well as retailing and e-commerce.


Outside of work, I spend most of my time on sport and fitness, reading, cooking fine dinners with my friends, travelling and experiencing new cultures. My next goal is to learn a third language, probably Spanish.
